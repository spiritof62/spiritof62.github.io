Bitmap Scramble


Congratulations - you unscrambled the image!

To play the game, load a bitmap image from your device into the large box on the left using the 'Choose File' button. Square images work best - if the image isn't square, then a square area will be extracted from the image for use in the game. Or instead of using the 'Choose File' button, you can drag and drop from your desk top or from the sample gallery of album cover images below. Whatever way it gets there, once an image is loaded into the large box, it will be scrambled into the tiles on the right - drag and drop these tiles to put the image back together!

Important: Currently only the bitmap file format (.bmp) is supported - other formats will not work.
